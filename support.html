Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a country I've visited?

Tap the + button on the Home or World tab, search for a country, then add a photo, rating, highlights, and a note. You can log the season and date of each visit, and revisit a country as many times as you like.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. When you sign in with Apple or Google, your trips, postcards, and photos sync securely to the cloud, so your travel story is safe even if you switch devices.

Can I use the app without an account?

Absolutely. You can explore as a guest. When you're ready, link your account with Apple or Google from the Profile tab to back up your data — nothing you've added is lost in the process.

How do I unlock badges?

Badges unlock automatically as you travel — for reaching country milestones, exploring new continents, collecting photos and memories, and more. Open the Trophies tab and tap any badge to see how to earn it.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to the Profile tab and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your trips, postcards, photos, and profile from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Which devices are supported?

Postcards runs on iPhone with iOS 18 or later.

Privacy

Postcards stores the travel data you choose to add (countries, photos, notes, and ratings) in your private account. We don't sell your data. For questions about your data, use the email above.
